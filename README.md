# Disguise Chat

Disguise Chat is a privacy-first hidden messaging app designed for people who want to keep personal conversations discreet during everyday phone use.

Unlike traditional messaging apps that are immediately recognizable on a home screen, Disguise Chat appears as a fully functional calculator. Only users who know the Secret PIN can access private conversations.

## Why Disguise Chat?

Many people occasionally hand their phones to friends, family members, or coworkers. Even when the phone is unlocked for a legitimate reason, visible messaging apps and conversation lists can reveal more than intended.

Disguise Chat helps reduce unwanted attention by combining a familiar calculator interface with privacy-focused features.

## Features

* Calculator disguise
* Secret PIN access
* Panic Code
* Anonymous accounts (no phone number or email required)
* End-to-end encryption
* Auto-lock when minimized or inactive

## Privacy Benefits

* Helps keep conversations out of plain sight
* Reduces attention to private chats
* Designed for everyday privacy scenarios
* Simple and familiar user experience
